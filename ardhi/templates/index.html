Ardhi System


(LIS) is a geographic information system for cadastral and land use mapping, typically used by local governments.
An LIS consists of an accurate, current and reliable land record cadastre and its associated attribute and spatial
data that represent the legal boundaries of land tenure and provides a vital base layer capable of integration into
other geographic systems or as a standalone solution that allows data stewards to retrieve, create, update, store, view,
analyze and publish land information.
